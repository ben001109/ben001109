I start from concrete user workflows and turn complex processes into clear, usable product experiences.

My current interests include:

- Interactive web and visual learning

## 🚀 Selected work

### ⛏️ [Image to Litematic](https://github.com/ben001109/image-to-litematic-tool)

<code>Python</code> · <code>PyQt5</code> · <code>Minecraft creator tools</code>

> Converts images into Minecraft .litematic schematics, with a focus on survival-obtainable blocks.

### 🎉 [Editable Birthday Giftcard](https://github.com/ben001109/editable-birthday-giftcard)

<code>JavaScript</code> · <code>Clerk</code> · <code>Supabase</code> · <code>Web Audio API</code>

### 🤖 [Binance Auto Trader](https://github.com/ben001109/Binance-Auto-Trader) — research prototype
